    Materials That Can Be Recycled Curbside Include:

  • Paper (newspaper, envelopes, etc)
  • Plastic that has a number 1-7 in a triangle (water bottles, milk jugs, etc)
    *not plastic bags*
  • Cardboard (Cereal boxes, toilet and paper towel rolls, etc)
  • Metal (Soup cans, clean tin foil, etc)
  • Glass (wine bottles, pasta sauce jars, etc)

    What Cannot Be Recycled Curbside:

  • Food or Garbage
  • Yard Waste
  • Styrofoam
  • Grocery, Chip, or Sandwich Bags
  • Paper Plates and Plastic Utensils
  • Napkins and Paper Towel
  • Electronics, Computers, Lightbulbs

    What Can Go to Recycle Here! -
    1331 Holden St, Detroit, MI 48202

  • Plastic Bags
  • Light Bulbs
  • Batteries
  • Styrofoam
  • Electronics
  • Cartons
  • Cooking Grease

    How to Recycle!

  • Materials should be thrown loosely in curbside bin
  • Nothing should be separated or placed in a bag
  • Empty and rinse out all containers before recycling
  • All items must be in the cart to be collected, not on top of or around it.
  • When in doubt, throw it out!
