Welcome to Recycling Challenge!

Drag each item to the corresponding container or place.
Get as many correct items as possible to win a FREE recycling container.

    Recycle these items curbside in your blue cart:

  • Paper (newspaper, envelopes, etc)
  • Plastic that has a number 1-7 in a triangle (water bottles, milk jugs, etc)
    *No plastic bags*
  • Cardboard (cereal boxes, toilet and paper towel rolls, etc)
  • Metal (soup cans, clean tin foil, etc)
  • Glass (soda bottles, pasta sauce jars, etc.)

    Do not put these items in your blue cart:

  • Food or Garbage
  • Yard Waste
  • Styrofoam
  • Grocery, Chip, or Sandwich Bags
  • Paper Plates and Plastic Utensils
  • Napkins and Paper Towel
  • Electronics, Computers, Lightbulbs

    Take these items to Recycle Here! - 5960 Lincoln St.

  • Light Bulbs
  • Batteries
  • Styrofoam
  • Electronics

    How to use your blue cart:

  • Materials should be thrown loosely in your cart
  • Do not bag or separate your items
  • Empty and rinse out all containers
  • All items must be inside the cart, not on top of or around it.
  • When in doubt, throw it out!
